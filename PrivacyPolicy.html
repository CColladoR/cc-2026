Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. The Save Image As extension is designed to be completely private, secure, and transparent.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data. The extension does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Local Processing

All image conversions are performed locally on your computer using the Chrome Offscreen API and HTML5 Canvas. Your images are never uploaded to any server or shared with third parties.

Permissions Explained

  • contextMenus: Used to add the "Save Image As" option to your right-click menu.
  • downloads: Used to save the converted image file to your computer.
  • offscreen: Used to perform the image conversion in a background process without interrupting your browsing.
  • host_permissions: Required to access image data from different websites to perform the conversion (CORS).
